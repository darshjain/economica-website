ARTHOTSAV is the Economics Fest organized by ‘ECONOMICA’- The Economics Department of D.G.Ruparel College. It is a student-volunteer initiative that focuses on the development via team building activities since 2005. The department has been imparting the knowledge of economics through several activities conducted every year including our annual intercollegiate fest - ‘ARTHOTSAV’. Economica inspires the students to work hard so that they can aspire to thrive in the practical world. This is why the department proudly stands with its motto: INSPIRE. ASPIRE. THRIVE. Arthotsav is one of the most celebrated and prestigious fests of The D. G. Ruparel College of Arts, Science and Commerce. Arthotsav has been conducted annually since 2011. It provides opportunities to acquire leadership skills, explore one’s creativity, and most importantly cooperation and teamwork. Participants revisit their classroom teachings and test their abilities in the simulated environment. Arthotsav tries to cover all the core aspects of economic programmes and so the experience becomes a solid foundation for one’s career.
