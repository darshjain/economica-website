The economics department of D.G.Ruparel College of Arts, Science, and Commerce renamed as ‘ECONOMICA’ in 2005, is today a proud parent to many events and activities. Beyond textbook lessons and notebook calculations, the students are given a taste of the outside world through many active programmes. Progressing steadily with the help of not only experienced but also inspiring professors Economica strives to deliver only the best to the students. Economica’s motto being ‘Inspire. Aspire. Thrive.’, it truly educates students more than any degree can offer. As the Head of Economica, Mrs. Anita Hamand says, ‘Economica is not only a department, it is a family which endeavours learning together.
